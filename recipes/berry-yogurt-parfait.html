Berry Yogurt Parfait

Description

Quick and healthy snack for those craving a sweeter morning delight!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup vanilla yogurt
  • ½ cup fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • ¼ cup granola
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional)

Steps

  1. Spoon half of the yogurt into a glass or bowl.
  2. Add half of the berries and a sprinkle of granola.
  3. Repeat with remaining yogurt, berries, and granola.
  4. Drizzle with honey, if desired.
