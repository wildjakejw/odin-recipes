Garlic Butter Green Beans

Description

Easy garlic butter green beans that makes the perfect side for your holiday occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh green beans, trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and cook the green beans for 4-5 minutes, until crisp-tender.
  2. Drain and set aside.
  3. Melt the butter in a skillet over medium heat, then add the garlic.
  4. Cook the garlic for about 30 seconds, until fragrant.
  5. Add the green beans, toss to coat, and season with salt and pepper.
